Popular Destinations

Winter Gulmarg

Gulmarg in winter is blanketed in snow, making it the perfect time to visit and indulge in skiing adventures.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam, Kashmir, nestled in the serene Kashmir Valley, offers breathtaking vistas and adventurous escapades amidst its lush greenery and snow-capped peaks.

Aharbal

Aharbal, Kashmir, a hidden gem in the majestic Kashmir Valley, captivates with its pristine landscapes and cascading waterfalls, promising a tranquil escape into nature's embrace.

Sonmarg

Sonmarg, Kashmir, a jewel in the crown of the Himalayas, enchants with its snow-capped peaks and meandering rivers, offering a glimpse of paradise amidst nature's grandeur.

Dal Lake

Dal Lake, Srinagar, is a mesmerizing jewel of Kashmir, where serene waters mirror the majestic mountains, offering an enchanting retreat amidst floating gardens and historic houseboats.

Dodhpathri

Dodhpathri, a serene paradise nestled in the heart of Kashmir, boasts lush meadows and pristine landscapes, inviting travelers to unwind amidst nature's tranquility and breathtaking beauty.

Nishat

Nishat Bagh, a marvel of Mughal architecture in Srinagar, Kashmir, captivates with its terraced gardens and panoramic views of the Dal Lake, offering a serene escape into timeless beauty.

Pari Mahal

Pari Mahal, nestled atop the Zabarwan Range in Srinagar, Kashmir, is a historic marvel adorned with terraced gardens and stunning views of the city below, offering a serene haven for reflection and relaxation.

Shalimar

Shalimar Bagh, a masterpiece of Mughal architecture nestled in Srinagar, Kashmir, enchants with its cascading terraces, tranquil water channels, and vibrant flower beds, inviting visitors to bask in its timeless beauty and serenity.

Tulip Garden

The Tulip Garden in Srinagar, Kashmir, stands as Asia's largest, captivating visitors with its vibrant hues and fragrant blooms, offering a fleeting yet unforgettable glimpse of nature's artistry.

Verinag

Verinag, nestled in the heart of Kashmir, is home to the enchanting Verinag Spring, a natural wonder revered for its crystal-clear waters and serene surroundings, offering a tranquil escape amidst nature's pristine beauty.

Amarnath

Amarnath, nestled in the sacred Himalayas of Kashmir, is revered as the abode of Lord Shiva, drawing pilgrims from far and wide to embark on a spiritual journey amidst majestic snow-capped peaks and serene valleys.

Baisaran Pahalgam

Baisaran, nestled in the picturesque Pahalgam region of Kashmir, is a hidden gem renowned for its lush meadows and breathtaking views of the surrounding Himalayan peaks, offering a tranquil retreat amidst nature's pristine beauty.

Chashmeshahi

Chashme Shahi, nestled in the serene hills of Kashmir, is a testament to Mughal grandeur, featuring terraced gardens, cascading fountains, and panoramic views of the Dal Lake, offering a tranquil oasis of beauty and relaxation.

Dachigam National Park

Dachigam National Park, nestled in the pristine mountains of Kashmir, is a sanctuary for rare wildlife species, including the majestic Kashmir stag, offering nature enthusiasts an unparalleled opportunity to witness the region's rich biodiversity amidst breathtaking natural landscapes.

Houseboat Dal Lake

These floating abodes, adorned with intricate woodwork and luxurious amenities, provide a serene retreat amidst picturesque surroundings, promising moments of tranquility and enchantment on the water.

Manasbal Lake

Surrounded by lush greenery and snow-capped peaks, this pristine lake offers visitors a peaceful retreat amidst nature's splendor, with opportunities for boating and birdwatching, making it a haven for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Naranag

Naranag offers visitors a serene retreat amidst the breathtaking landscapes of the region, along with a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of Kashmir.

Wullar Lake

Wular Lake provides a tranquil escape amidst nature's pristine beauty, perfect for boating, birdwatching, and leisurely strolls along its scenic shores.

Shankarcharya Temple

Shankaracharya Temple, perched atop Shankaracharya Hill in Srinagar, Kashmir, is a revered Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Harwan

Harwan, situated on the outskirts of Srinagar in Kashmir, is renowned for its scenic beauty and historical significance.
